Bus Stops are on almost every street and are too numerous to map. Metrobus fare for regular routes is $2.00 using a SmarTrip® card or cash. The fare for express routes is $4.25 using a SmarTrip® card or cash. Fare for seniors and people with disabilities is $1.00 for regular routes, $2.10 on express routes. You must have exact change.

This map can help you navigate through the D.C. area! Navigate the Map using the mouse.

On the Right are all the different locations we have marked for you!

The Checkmarks next to the marker types will hide or show that group.

Clicking on the group name will expand or condense the locations within that group.

The Directions tab can be used to click on markers and give specific directions to get from one to the other.
